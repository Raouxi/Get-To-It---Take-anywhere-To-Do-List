# Get-To-It---Take-anywhere-To-Do-List
A simple device for getting shit done. No more lost to-do lists or to-do lists lost in your phone. The Get To It allows you to always have your to-do list accessible to get all that stuff you've been meaning to complete done and finished

This to-do list tool can be manufactured and assembled easily from a couple of parts. Uses 3" x 3" standard Post-It notes cut to size to fit the tool which utilizes 2" x 3" Post-

3D Printed Parts:
1. Top Plate - holds the Post-It notes from not fallig out - qty 1
2. Middle Plate - base that the Post-It notes sit on - qty 1
3. Bottom Plate - holds the magnetic system to connect to phone, fridge, night stand, and more - qty 1

Hardware Needed:
M3x10mm SHCS - qty 4
M3 Nuts - qty 4
Adhesive Magnetic Ring - qty 1
Cherry MX Black Keyboard Spring - qty 4

Instructions:
1. Push the M3 nuts into place on the bottom plate
2. Cut the 3" x 3" Post-It notes down to 2" x 3" using a paper cutter or xacto knife while keeping the 2" at the top as the sticky side
3. Place the 4 springs into the opening on the bottom plate
4. Place the middle plate on the 4 springs so that the openings on the 4 springs align with nobs on the middle plate
5. Place the Post-It notes on the top of the middle plate
6. Place the top plate over the middle plate, Post-It note, and spring assembly and screw in the 4 M3 SHCS in the four corners of the Get To It tool
